Screening Questions

Your answers to the following questions would help us to understand which of our services may be of interest to you.
The questions are split in two groups: a) the questions focusing on the Self-Directed Research Tools and b) the questions focusing on Assisted Research Solutions.

Block A: Self-Directed Research Tools

Would you be interested in a personalised community for your brand where you could ask them any question relevant to your brand, future and ongoing projects?

Would you be interested in a quantitative tool to validate some of your findings with 500 people at once and get answers within 24 hours?

Would you be interested in a tool to directly connect with a group of your customers to learn why they buy your products, how they use them, and the feedback they have for you?
learn more



Would you be interested in a tool recruit specific individuals to interact with your products and provide you with feedback or engage in the other tasks? For instance, working with potential customers or rejectors to improve your product or inform your current marketing strategy.

Would you be interested in having our researchers & strategists organize and conduct open innovation projects for you, where engineers, designers, and industry experts reflect on the ways in your products and services may be improved?

Would you be interested having our researchers & strategists assess your organizational processes to uncover the unretrieved development opportunities?

Would you be interested in receiving a customized yearly report that tracks shifts in consumer needs, behaviours, and disruptions in your given industry?
